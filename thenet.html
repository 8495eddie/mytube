biker boyz

Biker Boyz takes you on an action-packed thrill-ride with some of the most extreme action stunts on wheels! Laurence Fishburne [The Matrix] is Smoke, the "King of Cali" - the undefeated and undisputed racing champion. His dominance and title are challenged by Kid [Derek Luke, Antwone Fisher], a fierce young racing prodigy, in this electrifying story of modern-day daredevils in the ultimate race to win the survival of the fastest. Featuring Orlando Jones, Kid Rock, Lisa Bonet and a hot, sexy, supporting cast, Biker Boyz is an explosive and heart-stopping edge-of-your-seat ride!
